By Alfredo Morales

I wasn't too sure which satisified the requierements of asymmetrical balance, so I decided to upload both. I did my best to achieve a precept that states that a large form is visually heavier than a small form. I believe I achieved this with the basketball in image 1 and the large trees in image 2. I decided to do a spin on the see-saw image that is usually used to demonstrate asymmetrical balance and give it my own twist by adding a third dimensional shape and using some pen tools. For the second image I went more freelancing and tried a paint=inspired type of image, in a surprisingly pleasing yet amateurish type of way.
