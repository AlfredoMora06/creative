A Copy-righted Great Adventure

Cuphead explores an rpg style game where the objective is to survive and avoid your enemy's attacks

By Alfredo Morales

This interactive game-based story involves navigating cuphead through a plain board. Inspired by pokemon and other rpg type games. You play as Cuphead who just happened to be walking through grasslands minding his own business when he encounters King Dice who is shooting cannnoballs at him due to the results of their previous encounters. It is up to you to survive this frenzy, whether you are able to or not will decide what ending Cuphead recieves.
