Angry Eye

It is what we do with anger once we feel it that matters most.

By Alfredo Morales

Anger is important in some cases. We need to have the internal recognition of knowing we have been wronged and that sometimes needs anger for us to realize it. Anger also is needed for people to be pushed to act.

It is what we do with anger once we feel it that matters most.

I ussed Illustrator to show an anger emotion. Accomplished this by using the circle, and square shape, the straight line and arc lines and a few colors for embezzlement. The eye looks mor cartoon-like but still conveys the specified emotion.
