Muerte

Eternity is not an infinite duration of time but rather it's timelessness...

By Alfredo Morales

Ernest Becker affirms that the human is the only being alive that is conscious of his own death. The only one who has the ability to imagine a world without him present, and that this realization has the potential to turn him into a neurotic if he does not solve it.

Maybe we should choose to live in the present and explore as many roads as possible with the time we have, as we well know... an unexplored life does not deserve to be lived. And perhaps eternity is not an infinite duration of time but rather it's timelessness.
