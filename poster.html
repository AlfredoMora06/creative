i-Art Poster

Polygons all the way down

By Alfredo Morales

For this assignment, everything came naturally. I used a Gotye music video as inspiration and decided to use the line tool for previous classes and the grid, to make use of polygons and align them up in a puzzle-esque style.

Polygons all the way down

For the typography I had to experiment and choose one to match the styling of my triangles and shapes. I also knew I wanted to add certain text into each shape so determining what would go where was definitely an interesing process.
