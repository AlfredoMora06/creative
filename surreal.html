A Horrific Hallway

Some shreak in awe, pay no mind, and others choose to laugh

Come in and explore your horrors!

By Alfredo Morales

For this exercise I focused on bringing some fun ideas I had in my head to life. I wanted to experiment with having people stuck in a bottle (probably a generic idea, but nonetheless interesting in implementing). People tend to express being stuck in a situation with incoming danger in different ways. Some shreak in awe as they observe, some freeze, some choose to not focus on it, some run and some laugh at it or at others misfortune. I used a clearer blending mode as well as darkened some spots with the pen tool to give the impression of shadows. I also used the select mask tool to cut out my images.

Some shreak in awe, pay no mind, and others choose to laugh

For the second image, I had recently watched Sinister and came up with the idea to make a horror concept. Perhaps a horror hallway... I had fun with this, applying the same concepts from before. However, as can be seen from the final image, I went for a darker blen mode for this. The images for all the layers for both final psd's were taken from Unsplash.
