A Walk Through Propspect Park

Symmetry and parallelism

By Alfredo Morales

This image was taken on Friday, February 19, 2021. I chose this image in particular for display because I was captivated by this symmetric path I was walking on while traversing the snowy day in Prospect Park. When looking around through the park, I knew I wanted to capture the beauty of simplicity and maybe a mix of nature with man made paths/structures. There is definitely something enchanted as well about this path that made me look more into it. Symmetry and parallelism is the composition I was looking for when thinking about composition. I added some exposure and lowered saturation to give it a more enchanted feel (walking through the woods).
